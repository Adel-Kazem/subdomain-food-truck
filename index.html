Our Story

Rolling Feast was born from a passion for great food and a desire to bring gourmet culinary experiences to the streets. Our chef-driven menu features locally sourced ingredients transformed into globally inspired dishes.

We believe food should be an adventure, which is why we change locations and offer new specials regularly. Follow us on social media to stay updated on where we'll be serving next!

Chef Michael Rodriguez

Head Chef & Founder
